Our Story

Mariana is the owner and the lead designer of Mangata and Gallo.
As she graduated from design school with a specialization in diamond
cutting and metal smithing, she found that this was truly her passion
and proceeded to create Managata and Gallo. Paying homage to her roots,
Mariana opened a storefront in her hometown, Austin. Recently, she hired
several artisans to help craft her designs and a young employee
to help manage the company website and social media accounts.
